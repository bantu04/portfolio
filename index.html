Crashworthiness of EV Battery Module

Challenge: Ensure battery integrity under high-velocity impact scenarios.

Solution: Modeled a 110kg battery tray in SolidWorks and simulated impact in LS-Dyna. Applied boundary conditions to mimic real-world crash loads.

Result: Optimized enclosure geometry reduced side impact deflection by 96% (256mm to 9.3mm). Validated structural integrity at 13.86 mm/ms.
